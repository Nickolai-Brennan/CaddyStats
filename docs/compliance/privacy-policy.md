# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026-05-13

## Purpose

This policy defines how Caddy Stats collects, uses, stores, protects, and deletes personal data across public, member, subscriber, editorial, and admin workflows.

## Data We Collect

- account and profile data (name, email, role, subscription state)
- authentication and session data
- billing and transaction metadata
- usage and analytics events
- support and communication records
- AI/editorial workflow metadata required for governance

## How We Use Data

- provide and secure platform services
- enforce entitlements and role-based access
- improve product quality, reliability, and usability
- support billing, fraud prevention, and account recovery
- maintain auditability and legal/compliance obligations

## Data Sharing

- with required infrastructure and payment providers under contract
- with analytics providers under approved operational controls
- when required by law, legal process, or valid regulatory request

Caddy Stats does not sell personal data as a standalone product.

## Security Controls

- JWT authentication and role-based access controls
- least-privilege access for internal tools and data systems
- secret management through environment controls
- audit logging for privileged actions
- redaction requirements for sensitive logs

## User Rights and Requests

Where required by applicable law, users may request:

- access to their personal data
- correction of inaccurate data
- deletion of eligible personal data
- export of eligible account data
- restriction or objection where applicable

## Retention and Deletion

Retention windows and deletion controls are defined in:

- `docs/compliance/data-retention-policy.md`

## Policy Changes

Material policy changes must be documented, versioned, and reflected in project changelog updates.
