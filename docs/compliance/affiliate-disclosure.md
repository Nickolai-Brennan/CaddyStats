# Affiliate Disclosure

Last Updated: 2026-05-13

## Purpose

This disclosure defines how Caddy Stats presents affiliate relationships in content, tools, and monetized user journeys.

## Disclosure Rule

When affiliate links, offers, or partner promotions are present, Caddy Stats must provide clear and proximate disclosure.

## Minimum Disclosure Requirements

- identify that compensation may be received from partner links or actions
- place disclosure near relevant content or call-to-action locations
- ensure disclosures are visible on mobile and desktop experiences
- avoid misleading language implying guaranteed outcomes or approvals

## Editorial Independence

- affiliate relationships must not override grounding, provenance, or review standards
- unsupported betting claims remain prohibited even in affiliate contexts
- disclosures must be consistent with the stat-grounding policy and editorial controls

## Operational Controls

- track affiliate placements and disclosure coverage in content templates
- include affiliate disclosure checks in editorial review workflows
- document partner policy constraints where required by contract
