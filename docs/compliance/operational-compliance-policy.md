# Operational Compliance Policy

Last Updated: 2026-05-13

## Purpose

This policy defines operational controls for ongoing compliance across privacy, disclosures, retention, AI governance, and editorial integrity.

## Control Areas

- policy ownership and review cadence
- validation gates in delivery workflows
- changelog and evidence requirements
- incident and exception handling
- vendor and partner compliance checks

## Ownership and Review Cadence

- compliance policy owner: platform/governance owner
- domain co-owners: security, data, editorial, and product owners
- formal review cadence: quarterly minimum, plus ad hoc on material changes

## Validation Gate Expectations

Material changes affecting user data, disclosures, or public claims should confirm:

- policy alignment before implementation
- required approvals for high-risk domains
- updated documentation and changelog entries
- deployment readiness and rollback awareness

## Evidence and Recordkeeping

- maintain changelog traceability for policy-relevant changes
- preserve review evidence for major compliance-impacting releases
- retain audit events for privileged actions and sensitive workflows

## Incident and Exception Handling

- document policy exceptions with scope, rationale, owner, and expiry
- log compliance incidents with corrective and preventive actions
- track unresolved compliance debt with explicit remediation ownership
