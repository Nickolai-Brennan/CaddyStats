# Editorial Intelligence Strategy

## Objective

Create an editorial system that scales golf publishing through structure, analytics, and AI assistance while preserving trust and reviewability.

## Editorial intelligence model

Editorial intelligence means combining:

- structured blocks and templates
- verified stat insertion and model context
- metadata and internal linking systems
- AI assistance with grounding and review controls
- workflow visibility across draft, review, publish, and refresh states

## Strategic priorities

- reduce manual research and publishing friction
- keep editorial claims tied to source data and freshness signals
- make high-volume page generation possible without turning pages into filler
- preserve an approval trail for sensitive or premium content

## Required capabilities

- reusable templates for recurring golf page types
- clear separation between narrative content and computed values
- article versioning and review state tracking
- attribution for AI-assisted output and edits
- publishing controls that trigger cache and search updates safely
