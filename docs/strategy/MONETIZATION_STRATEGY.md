# Monetization Strategy

## Objective

Turn differentiated golf research utility into recurring revenue without damaging user trust.

## Revenue streams

- premium subscriptions for deeper workflow access
- affiliate revenue from trusted, qualified referrals
- long-term data differentiation that strengthens subscription value

## Monetization principles

- premium value should unlock depth and workflow speed, not remove the minimum trust-building free experience
- acquisition should come from useful public pages and clear education, not hype
- pricing and packaging should reinforce repeat weekly utility
- entitlement behavior must be enforced server-side and remain auditable

## Value ladder

1. public discovery through SEO and readable analytics
2. account creation for activation and habit formation
3. premium conversion when users hit advanced workflow needs
4. retention through recurring tournament-week utility, explainability, and operational trust

## Core metrics

- visitor-to-signup conversion
- free-to-paid conversion
- subscriber retention and renewal
- ARPU and LTV trends
- affiliate conversion from qualified surfaces
