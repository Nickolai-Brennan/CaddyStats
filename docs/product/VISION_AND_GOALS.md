# Vision and Goals

## Vision

Build the most trusted golf analytics and betting intelligence platform by combining transparent data systems, explainable models, and scalable editorial execution.

## Mission

Turn fragmented golf research into a unified, auditable, and monetizable product ecosystem for bettors, fantasy users, analysts, and editorial teams.

## Product goals

### User value goals
- deliver reliable player, course, and tournament insights
- make model output understandable and reviewable
- support fast weekly research workflows for betting and fantasy preparation
- keep public and premium experiences readable, not just technically rich

### Platform goals
- preserve PostgreSQL as the source of truth
- maintain clean boundaries across stats, content, auth, billing, and audit concerns
- stabilize API contracts before tightly coupling UI experiences to implementation detail
- keep AI outputs grounded, reviewable, and logged

### Business goals
- grow qualified organic traffic
- convert anonymous visitors into members and subscribers
- increase recurring subscription revenue
- expand affiliate opportunities tied to verified decision support

## Strategic principles

1. Data first.
2. Explainability over hype.
3. Architecture before implementation sprawl.
4. Premium value must be tangible.
5. Security and governance are part of the product.
6. Editorial speed cannot come at the cost of trust.
