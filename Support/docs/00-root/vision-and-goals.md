# Caddy Stats — Vision and Goals

Version: 1.0
Status: Active
Last Updated: 2026-05-12

---

## 1. Vision

Build the most trusted golf analytics and betting intelligence platform by combining transparent data systems, explainable models, and scalable editorial execution.

---

## 2. Mission

Turn fragmented golf research into a unified, auditable, and monetizable product ecosystem for bettors, fantasy users, and data-driven golf audiences.

---

## 3. Product Goals

### 3.1 User Value Goals

- Deliver reliable player, course, and tournament insights
- Make projection logic understandable and inspectable
- Provide practical research workflows for betting and fantasy prep
- Maintain strong readability for public and premium content

### 3.2 Platform Goals

- Keep PostgreSQL as source of truth
- Maintain clean `content` and `stats` boundaries
- Ship API contracts before deep UI coupling
- Keep AI features grounded, reviewable, and logged

### 3.3 Business Goals

- Grow qualified organic traffic via SEO content systems
- Convert users from anonymous to registered to paid
- Increase recurring subscription revenue
- Expand affiliate opportunities tied to verified analysis

---

## 4. Non-Goals (Current Phase)

- Publishing unsupported betting claims
- Expanding to broad multi-sport coverage before golf platform maturity
- Optimizing vanity metrics over retention and subscriber value
- Relying on AI-generated content without structured grounding

---

## 5. Success Metrics (Directional)

- Engagement depth on analytics and research pages
- Free-to-paid subscription conversion
- Subscriber retention and renewal behavior
- Content velocity with quality controls preserved
- API and dashboard performance for high-read analytics paths

---

## 6. Strategic Principles

1. **Data First** — no fabricated stats or unsupported claims.
2. **Architecture First** — preserve system boundaries and build order.
3. **Transparency First** — distinguish model output from verified source data.
4. **Security First** — protect access, sanitize content, and avoid secret leakage.
5. **Monetization Alignment** — prioritize work that improves revenue, retention, or operational leverage.
